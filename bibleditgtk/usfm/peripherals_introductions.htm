\id INT

The INT book and its divisions can be used for storing introductory material related to the various book groupings within a scripture publication. At times this material has also been referred to as "mid-matter". The following USFM markers are recommended for use in Introduction content.

 

·\mt# - Main title.
·\s# - Section heading.
·\p - Paragraph.
·\m - Flush left (margin) paragraph.
·\pi# - Indented paragraph.
·\li# - List item.
·\q# - Poetic line.
·\tr, \th#, \thr#, \tc#, \tcr# - For any tabular data.
