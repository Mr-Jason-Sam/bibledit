Outline

Bibledit shows the outline of the book that is being edited.

The outline can be seen in the Tools Area under the tab "Outline".

All the headers and titles that are in the book are visible there, with the chapters and verses they start with and end with.

The header or title that has the cursor will be focused.

If you double-click on any header, or press Enter, the chapter belonging to that will be loaded in the editor.

Notes.

- Headers and titles that are in chapter 0, that means, before chapter 1, do not show up as such, but as chapter 0 without any verse-references.

- There are no heading endmarkers if the heading is followed by another one.
