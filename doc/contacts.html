Contacts

There are various people who are involved in Bibledit. You can communicate with them through the bibledit mailinglist.

Subscribe to the bibledit mailing list
Email:

To subscribe to this list by email, send email to bibledit-subscribe@googlegroups.com. To unsubscribe send mail to bibledit-unsubscribe@googlegroups.com. Alternatively you can visit the group and manage your subscription at http://groups.google.com/group/bibledit. Archives can be viewed there too.

Bugs and feature requests can be submitted at http://sourceforge.net/projects/bibledit.

Whoever is bold enough to contact the programmer directly, his name will be subscribed to the bibledit list and his message will be forwarded to that list, to be answered from there. This is so that everybody can profit and contribute, and the programmer be relieved.
